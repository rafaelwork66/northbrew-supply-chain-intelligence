# Project Charter

## Objective
Reduce supplier-related service failures and inventory working capital without weakening
customer availability.

## Stakeholders
Sponsor: COO. Decision owners: Procurement Manager, Supply Chain Manager and CFO.
Users: buyers, inventory planners, warehouse managers and finance analysts.

## In scope
Purchase orders, supplier reliability, inbound freight, defects, inventory and stockouts.

## Out of scope
Customer-level pricing, detailed transport routing and production planning.

## Success measures
Raise on-time delivery, reduce stockout days, improve fill rate and reduce average inventory
value while maintaining service.
